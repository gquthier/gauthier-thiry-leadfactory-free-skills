# Strategy And Creative Brief

Use this after the three DeepSearch reports and Meta Ads Library CSV exist.

## Strategy Document

Save as `03-strategy/strategy.md`.

Required sections:

1. Executive summary.
2. Offer and target recap.
3. Awareness level and market sophistication.
4. Core positioning.
5. Main buying triggers.
6. Objections and proof needed.
7. Competitor angle map.
8. Saturated angles to avoid or twist.
9. White spaces to test.
10. Meta Ads test structure:
   - Campaign objective.
   - 1 campaign by default.
   - 2 ad sets maximum: broad and interest/contextual if relevant.
   - 5-10 static creative concepts.
11. Angle matrix:
   - Angle.
   - Buyer belief targeted.
   - Hook.
   - Proof required.
   - CTA.
12. Launch risks and manual validation checklist.

Do not include internal KPI thresholds, spend promises, or guaranteed performance claims.

## Creative Brief

Save as `04-creative-brief/creative-brief.md`.

Required sections:

1. Brand and offer context.
2. Target audience.
3. Visual direction:
   - Format: static 1:1 and 4:5 by default.
   - Mood.
   - Typography style.
   - Color direction.
   - Imagery style.
4. Copy direction:
   - Tone.
   - Words to use.
   - Words to avoid.
   - Claims requiring proof.
5. 10 static creative concepts.

Each creative concept must include:
- Concept name.
- Angle.
- Format.
- On-image headline, max 8 words.
- Support line, max 15 words.
- Visual layout.
- Primary text idea, 1-3 sentences.
- CTA.
- Required proof or asset.

Keep copy strategic and concise. Do not write full VSL scripts or long ad script packs.

## CTA Placement

The CTA file is separate at `05-cta/work-with-leadfactory.md`. Do not make the whole delivery a sales page. Keep the main deliverables useful on their own.
