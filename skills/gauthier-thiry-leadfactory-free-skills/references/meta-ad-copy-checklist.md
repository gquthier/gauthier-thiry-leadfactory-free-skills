# Meta Ad Copy Checklist

Use this checklist for strategy and static creative concepts. Do not generate long video scripts.

## Before Writing Concepts

- The offer is understandable in one sentence.
- The target avatar is concrete.
- The traffic goal is one action.
- Proof is public, user-provided, or clearly marked as needed.
- Claims avoid guarantees unless the user can substantiate them.
- Competitor insights are used as pattern signals, not copied.

## Static Creative Concept Rules

- One idea per creative.
- One buyer belief per creative.
- One CTA per creative.
- On-image headline: max 8 words.
- Support line: max 15 words.
- Primary text idea: 1-3 sentences, not a full script.
- Mention proof requirement if the concept depends on proof.

## Angles To Include

Create 5-10 concepts across:
- Douleur.
- Desir.
- Preuve.
- ContreIntuitif.
- Urgence.
- Educatif.
- Comparaison.
- Autorite.
- Transformation.

## Avoid

- Full VSL scripts.
- Full face-camera scripts.
- Medical, financial, or legal guarantees.
- Personal attribute callouts that violate ad policy.
- "Before/after" claims in sensitive categories.
- Fake scarcity.
- Invented testimonials.
- Private client results.
